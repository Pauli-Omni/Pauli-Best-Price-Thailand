Omni Solutions Global — application & registry templates (placeholders only)
=============================================================================
These are NOT pre-filled legal filings. Insert values from your official DBD /
business-registration documents. Have counsel review before you submit anything.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
1) D-U-N-S Number (Dun & Bradstreet)
    Purpose: often required for Apple Developer / some enterprise partner checks.
    Where to start: https://www.dnb.com/duns-number/get-a-duns.html
    Attach / prepare: company legal name as on DBD extract, registered address,
    contact email/phone, VAT/tax IDs if asked.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
2) Apple Developer Program (iOS / App Store)
    Purpose: distribute an iOS app under your legal entity.
    Enrol: https://developer.apple.com/programs/enroll/
    Docs: https://developer.apple.com/documentation/
    Prepare: legal entity name, D-U-N-S if enrolling as organization, banking
    for fees, privacy policy URL on your domain.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
3) Google Play Console (Android)
    Purpose: distribute an Android app.
    Sign up: https://play.google.com/console/signup
    Policy centre: https://developer.android.com/google-play/guides
    Prepare: same legal identity story as Apple; privacy policy and data safety.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
4) Lazada — partner / seller / affiliate (choose the programme that matches)
    Thailand marketplace hub: https://www.lazada.co.th/
    Seller / partner entry is typically via Lazada Seller Center (regional).
    Search Lazadas official site for Seller Center or Affiliate for the
    latest Thailand intake — programmes and URLs change; do not rely on
    deep links from third parties.
